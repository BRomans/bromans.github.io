About Me

I am Michele Romani, and I come from a little town in the countryside of Northern Italy. As far as I remember, I have always been passionate about understanding how things work and why. Something clicked when my innate curiosity met a computer for the first time. It was not just a mere work tool, but an extension of my brain, and that allowed me to unleash my creativity and cultivate my ambitions like anything else before. In spite of that spirit, I decided that my efforts would be focused on using technology to develop applications that can improve the quality of life, by stimulating users' understanding of the self to enhance their experience and make technology pleasurable and meaningful. In my human-centred view of technology, the user's control over creativity and flow is the foundation of every project I work on. At the core, I see myself as a "designer", in the broad meaning of the word. When I develop software, when I do scientific research, when I build interfaces, when I organize teamwork or plan the next project roadmap, I am above anything else just designing. And I love it.
